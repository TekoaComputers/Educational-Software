Hotspots

Drag on the image to draw a rectangle. Each hotspot has three independent fields:
Click anim — FLI that plays when the user clicks (e.g. kid walks to the chair).
Route to — optional; the screen to navigate to after the click anim ends.
Return anim — optional; played when coming back from that route (e.g. kid walks back).
Coords are in original 320×200 image space.
